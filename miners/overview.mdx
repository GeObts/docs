If you have a Bitcoin ASIC at home — a Bitaxe, an S19, anything — BasedMining is built for you.

## What you get

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="0% pool fee" icon="percent">
    We don't take a cut of block rewards. Operator revenue is from Hashpower NFT sales — your mining is yours.
  </Card>

  <Card title="1 BTC if you find a block" icon="trophy">
    Chain-enforced. The Bitcoin network itself pays the finder reward directly to your wallet. Even the pool can't take it.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Steady share every round" icon="chart-line">
    When someone else finds a block, you still get a proportional share of the ~2.1 BTC remainder based on the work you did that round.
  </Card>

  <Card title="No minimum hashrate" icon="gauge-low">
    Run one Bitaxe or twenty S19s — the pool treats you the same.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works for you

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure your miner">
    Point your ASIC at the pool's stratum endpoint, set your BTC address as the worker username, and pick any worker name you like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start hashing">
    The pool sees your shares the moment they start arriving. You'll show up on the live dashboard within a minute or two.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Win or earn — either way">
    If your share solves a block, Bitcoin sends 1 BTC to your address automatically. If someone else's share solves it, the operator distributes your share of the remainder to your address.
  </Step>
</Steps>
