If you have a Bitcoin ASIC at home — an Apollo, a Bitaxe, an S19, anything — BasedMining is built for you.

## What you get

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Direct BTC payouts" icon="bitcoin-sign">
    Rewards are paid in BTC to the address you register. We don't custody your funds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No minimum hashrate" icon="gauge-low">
    Run one Bitaxe or twenty S19s — the pool treats you the same.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live stats per worker" icon="chart-line">
    Watch your hashrate, share count, and accepted/rejected ratio in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stratum-compatible" icon="plug">
    Standard stratum protocol — works with any compliant ASIC firmware.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works for you

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure your miner">
    Point your ASIC at the pool's stratum endpoint, set your BTC address as the worker username, and pick any worker name you like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start hashing">
    The pool sees your shares the moment they start arriving. You'll show up on the live dashboard within a minute or two.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get paid">
    When the pool finds blocks, your share of the reward is paid to the BTC address you registered.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you need before starting

<Check>A Bitcoin ASIC running compatible firmware (most modern firmware is fine — Braiins OS, vanilla, BraiinsOS+, ePIC, etc.)</Check>
<Check>A Bitcoin wallet address you control — never use an exchange deposit address</Check>
<Check>A wired internet connection (Wi-Fi works but is not recommended for ASICs)</Check>
