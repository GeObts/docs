## 2. Blockchain Data and Transparency

Because BasedMining operates on blockchain technology, certain information related to your transactions, including your digital wallet address, NFT ownership, mining contributions, and block payouts, will be recorded on public, immutable blockchain ledgers including the Base network and the Bitcoin network. This information cannot be altered or deleted once recorded. As a result, you should be aware that:

- Information stored on the blockchain is publicly accessible and may be viewed by anyone.
- We cannot delete or modify information recorded on the blockchain.
- Your Ethereum-based wallet address (used for NFT minting on Base) is linked to a Bitcoin address derived for your mining contributions. Both addresses are visible on their respective public blockchains.
- You are responsible for managing your digital wallet and the privacy of your wallet addresses.
- The nature of blockchain technology may limit our ability to fully comply with certain data deletion requests under data protection laws.
