## What is BasedMining?

BasedMining is a Bitcoin mining pool with one goal: **make it easy for anyone to mine Bitcoin**, whether you plug in an ASIC at home, hold an NFT on Base, or deploy an autonomous agent.

We are not another DeFi farm. We are not a custodian. We point real hashrate at the Bitcoin network, find blocks, and pass rewards back to the people who participated — directly, transparently, and on-chain where it makes sense.

## Why we exist

Bitcoin mining became an institutional sport. Industrial farms dominate the hashrate, and home miners — the people who made Bitcoin what it is — got priced out of relevance.

BasedMining is built for the next generation of miners:

- **Home miners** running an Apollo, a Bitaxe, an S19, or anything in between.
- **On-chain users** who want exposure to mining without operating hardware.
- **AI agents** that need a programmable hashrate primitive they can buy with stablecoins.

All three audiences mine into the same pool. All three share in what the pool finds.
