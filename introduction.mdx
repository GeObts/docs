## What is BasedMining?

BasedMining is a Bitcoin mining pool with one job: **make it easy for anyone to mine Bitcoin**. Plug in a rig at home, mint a Hashpower NFT on Base, or point an autonomous agent at us via x402 — same pool, same rewards, same shot at finding a block.

We're a **hybrid lottery pool**. When the pool finds a block, the miner whose share solved it gets **1 BTC** instantly, paid by the Bitcoin network itself. The **remaining ~2.1+ BTC + fees** is split proportionally among everyone who contributed work to that round. Nobody walks away with nothing.

**0% pool fee.** Our revenue is from NFT sales, not from your block rewards.

## Why this exists

Bitcoin mining is mostly industrial farms now. Home miners — the people who made Bitcoin what it is — got priced out of competing on raw hashrate.

But Bitcoin mining is still **the best lottery in the world**. A single home miner can — and occasionally does — solve a block. It's rare, but it happens, and when it does, that miner takes home the full coinbase. The math doesn't care if the rig is in a warehouse or a garage.

BasedMining is built around that fact. We pool hashpower across home miners, NFT-backed rentals, and agents — and during our **Block Parties** we concentrate even more hashpower into the same window. More hashpower aimed at the same target means more shots at a block. When one lands, the finder gets 1 BTC and everyone else who contributed gets a piece.
