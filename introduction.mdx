## What is BasedMining?

BasedMining is a Bitcoin mining pool with one goal: **make it easy for anyone to pool together and mine Bitcoin**, whether you plug in an ASIC at home, hold a hashpower NFT, or deploy your autonomous agent via x402 — same pool, same rewards, same shot at finding a block.

We are not another DeFi farm. We are not a custodian. We point real hash at the Bitcoin network, find blocks, and pass rewards back to the people who participated — directly, transparently, and onchain where it makes sense.

We're a **hybrid lottery pool**. When the pool finds a block, the miner whose share solved it gets **1 BTC** instantly, paid by the network itself. The **remaining ~2.1\+ BTC** is split proportionally among everyone who contributed work to that round. Every participant gets a payout. 

**0% pool fee.** You win...we win.

## Why this exists

Bitcoin mining became an institutional sport. Industrial farms are dominating the space, and home miners — the people who made Bitcoin what it is — are getting priced out of the competition. We are doing our part to fight back against centralization while adding in some fun.

- **Home miners** running an a Bitaxe, a fleet of S21's, or anything in between.
- **Onchain users** who want exposure to mining without operating hardware.
- **AI agents** that need a programmable hashrate primitive they can buy with stablecoins.

All three audiences mine into the same pool. All three share rewards when the pool finds a block.

Bitcoin mining is still **the best lottery in the world**. A single home miner can — and occasionally does — solve a block. It's rare, but it happens, and when it does, that miner takes home the full reward. The math doesn't care if the rig is in a warehouse or a garage or an AI.

BasedMining is built around that fact. We pool hashpower across home miners, NFT-backed rentals, and agents. During our **Block Parties** we concentrate hashpower into the same window. More hashpower aimed at the same target means more shots at a block. When one lands, the finder gets 1 BTC and everyone else who contributed gets a piece.
