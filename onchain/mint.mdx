## Before you start

<Check>A wallet that supports Base (Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, MetaMask, Frame, etc.)</Check>
<Check>A small amount of ETH on **Base Sepolia** for the testnet phase</Check>
<Check>About two minutes</Check>

<Note>
  We're on **Base Sepolia** testnet. ETH costs there are negligible — get some from any Base Sepolia faucet (Coinbase's faucet at [coinbase.com/faucets/base-ethereum-sepolia-faucet](https://coinbase.com/faucets/base-ethereum-sepolia-faucet) is the easiest).
</Note>

## Walkthrough

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the mint page">
    Open [basedmining.xyz/mint](https://basedmining.xyz/mint).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Click **Connect Wallet** in the top right. Approve the connection in your wallet popup.

    Make sure your wallet is on **Base Sepolia**. If it's on the wrong network, the site will prompt you to switch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how many to mint">
    Each BasedPool NFT represents one unit of pool participation. You can mint one, several, or many. The mint UI shows the price per NFT and the gas estimate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm in your wallet">
    Click **Mint**, review the transaction in your wallet, and confirm. The transaction takes a few seconds on Base.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See your NFT">
    Once confirmed, your NFT appears in your wallet and on the [dashboard](https://basedmining.xyz). Your share of pool rewards starts accruing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after minting

- **Reward accrual starts immediately.** Your share is proportional to the total NFTs minted and your hold count.
- **Transferable.** You can move your NFT to any other Base wallet, or list it on a Base-compatible NFT marketplace.
- **No lockup.** There is no minimum hold period. You can keep, transfer, or sell whenever you want.

## Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My wallet says insufficient funds">
    Even on testnet you need a tiny amount of Base Sepolia ETH for gas. Use a Base Sepolia faucet, then try again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The mint button is greyed out">
    Either you're not connected or you're on the wrong network. Reconnect and confirm you're on **Base Sepolia**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transaction failed">
    Most failed mints are gas-too-low or wrong-network. Double-check the network in your wallet, increase gas slightly, and retry.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I minted but I don't see my NFT in my wallet">
    Some wallets are slow to index new NFTs. Refresh the wallet, switch networks back and forth, or wait a few minutes. The NFT is yours regardless of whether your wallet UI has caught up.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
