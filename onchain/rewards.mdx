## Accrual

When the pool earns rewards, a portion is allocated to the on-chain pool, distributed across all BasedPool NFTs in proportion to holdings.

You don't have to do anything for accrual to happen. As long as you hold the NFT, your share grows.

## Claiming

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Go to [basedmining.xyz](https://basedmining.xyz) and connect your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your claimable balance">
    The dashboard shows your unclaimed rewards. If the balance is zero, no claim is available yet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Claim">
    The dashboard prompts your wallet for a transaction. Approve it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive on Base">
    Once the transaction confirms, the rewards land in your wallet on Base.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequency

You can claim whenever you want, but each claim costs gas (small amounts on Base, but non-zero). It's usually most economical to let rewards accrue for a while before claiming.

<Note>
  There is no expiry on rewards. You don't lose them if you wait.
</Note>

## What if I sell my NFT?

When you transfer or sell a BasedPool NFT, **future** reward accrual goes to the new owner. Rewards already accrued (and unclaimed) stay with the address that held the NFT during accrual — make sure to claim before transferring if you want to keep them.

## Tax and reporting

Rewards from BasedPool NFTs may be taxable in your jurisdiction. We do not provide tax advice. Keep records of mints, claims, and transfers — your wallet's transaction history on Base is the source of truth.
