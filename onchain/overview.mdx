You don't need an ASIC to participate in Bitcoin mining anymore.

BasedMining lets you mint a **Hashpower NFT** on **Base mainnet**, and your mint rents real spot-market hashpower that points at the pool during the next **Block Party**. When the pool finds a block, you get a proportional share of the remainder — paid in **cbBTC on Base**, automatically.

## What is a Hashpower NFT?

A Hashpower NFT is your onchain receipt for a specific amount of mining hashpower over a specific window of time.

## Block Parties

Hashpower NFTs activate during **Block Parties** — 24-hour windows where the pool concentrates extra hashpower into the same round. More hashpower aimed at the same target means more shots at finding a block, and more shots means more chances for the whole pool to win.

Every Hashpower NFT mint **auto-queues for the next Block Party**. You don't have to do anything. When the event starts, your rented hashpower activates and mines for the full window.

Missed this Party? Your mint queues for the next one — your time window starts when the Party starts.

## How payments work

Mint with **ETH**, **USDC**, or **cbBTC** — all on **Base mainnet**. The mint contract reads live price feeds and quotes the equivalent for whichever token you pick.

You can mint multiple NFTs in a single transaction (up to 20 per call). Quantity multiplies hashpower and time, not just price.

## What stays fixed vs. what varies

| Fixed | Varies |
| --- | --- |
| USD price per tier | Hashpower (PH/s) |
| Payout token (cbBTC) | Duration (hours) |
| Payout chain (Base) | Block-find timing (luck) |
| Distribution math (proportional) | Number of blocks during your window |

## Why we do it this way

Bitcoin mining is still **the best lottery in the world**. A single home miner *can* — and occasionally does — solve a block. We can't make you guaranteed money, but we can give you exposure to a Block Party where the whole pool is aiming at finding a block together. When one lands, the finder gets 1 BTC and everyone who contributed (you included) gets a slice of the ~2.1 BTC remainder.

The **more Hashpower NFTs you mint**, the bigger your contribution to that Block Party, and the bigger your slice if a block is found. It also moves you up the [leaderboards](/dashboard/leaderboards) where occasional bonus rewards land.
