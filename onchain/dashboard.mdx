The [BasedMining dashboard](https://basedmining.xyz) is the live view into the pool. Anyone can see the global stats; connecting your wallet personalizes it to show your participation.

## Public stats (no wallet needed)

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pool hashrate" icon="gauge">
    Live aggregated hashrate across all connected miners. Updates in near real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blocks found" icon="cube">
    Recent blocks the pool has found, with timestamps and block heights.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active miners" icon="users">
    How many distinct workers are connected right now.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pool fee" icon="percent">
    The current pool fee — published, not hidden.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Personalized view (wallet connected)

After you click **Connect Wallet** you also see:

- Your NFT holdings and minted count
- Your share of recent reward distributions
- Claimable rewards balance
- Reward history (each distribution and the txid)

## Per-miner page

If you mine to BasedMining with hardware, you also have a per-miner page:

```
basedmining.xyz/miner/<your-btc-address>
```

This page shows your worker(s), their hashrate, accepted vs rejected shares, and your BTC payout history.

<Tip>
  Bookmark your miner page. It's a clean, sharable URL — useful for posting screenshots and tracking your rig over time.
</Tip>

## Calculator

There's also a [calculator](https://basedmining.xyz/calculator) for quick estimates: enter a hashrate and it tells you roughly what to expect at current pool conditions. It's an estimate, not a guarantee — Bitcoin mining rewards are stochastic.
